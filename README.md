## About Me

I am a dedicated and enthusiastic researcher currently pursuing a Master of Science degree in Computer Science and Technology at Hohai University. I previously earned my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Intelligent Science and Technology from the same institution. My current research is centered on the cutting edge of artificial intelligence, with a particular focus on **Reinforcement Learning (RL)**, **Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL)**, and the **fine-tuning of Large Language Models (LLMs) utilizing RL techniques**. I am passionate about exploring novel methodologies to enhance the adaptability and efficiency of intelligent systems in complex, dynamic environments.
